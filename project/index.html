Inception

The application where you have applications



Welcome to Inception. Here you will find some useful and/or fun apps/games to pass the time with.


Ever wanted to play a casual game of Snake or Tic-Tac-Toe or anything else of the like? Well here, you can!


Below you can find whatever application you want to go on. If you scroll further you will find a form which you can fill out. You can suggest anything, give positive or negative feedback or anything else.
